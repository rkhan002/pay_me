Pay Me — Quick Rules

Contract Rummy for 2–8 players · 11 hands

Object: Be first to form your entire hand into melds.


Hands: Hand 1 = 3 cards … Hand 11 = 13.


Wilds: Jokers always wild. Wild-rank cards default to natural; use "Force as wild" to change. Max half of any meld can be wild. At least 2 natural cards per meld.


Melds: Set = 3–4 same rank, different suits. Run = 3+ consecutive same suit.


Turn: Draw from stock or discard, then discard one card.


Going out: Whole hand melds → "Pay Me!" Caller scores 0 ($). One final turn each. Then lay-off phase.


Scoring: Caller=$0. Others: Wild=0, A=15, K–10=10, 9–2=face value.
